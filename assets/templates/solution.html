Solutions

Work Health & Safety

Using OPTRON technology, HR Master assists colleagues in managing their work health and safety obligations by giving HR professionals a wide range of tools and reports that cover key areas of compliance.

HRM's asset and hazardous substance registers, allow colleagues to create secondary users with appropriate permissions, to create, track, update and set reminders for plant and equipment, personal protective equipment and hazardous substances within their workplace.

Based on HAZPAK standards, HRM's injury register gives colleagues the tools to not only record incidents and near misses, but generate an array of reports, metrical data and set key email and dashboard reminders to remind them of upcoming dates and events.

Training & Development

While small and medium businesses often have comprehensive one-on-one and paper based training systems in place, larger businesses are often required to utilise expensive on-line learning management systems to facilitate their training obligations under the legislation.

As a fully integrated HR Platform, HR Master not only has an inbuilt learning management system that enables colleagues to create courses, but also utilise global courses created by HRM staff and made available to all subscribers.

Colleagues creating their own courses, can upload documents, videos and images for training material, as well as create their own questions and answers. Once colleagues allocate those courses to their staff, they will receive correspondence advising them of their course login, making course allocation as simple as a click of a button. Colleagues can manage their employees record keeping and training obligations by utilising HRM's dashboard and reminder systems making HRM, a holistic HR package.

Industrial Relations

We live in a world of compliance. Work health and safety, industrial relations, and consumer laws alone, place significant and onerous obligations on colleagues to provide effective training and development.

Having decades of experience in Courts and Industrial Relations Tribunals/Commissions (both State and Federal), our award winning team have designed HRM, with employee registers that give colleagues both the tools to record employee and other interactions with privacy and confidentiality at the very core of the system.

In addition to allowing colleagues to record licenses and employee information, OPTRON technology facilitates HR diaries, flag alert and confidentiality flags to give you peace of mind when making file notes.

Only colleagues you authorise will be able to access and/or edit your employee employee file notes and alerts, giving you the ability to track time spent per department and generate important metrical data. By Utilising HRM's confidentiality flag, uploading documents and tagging other employees into various notes, key information can be recalled by authorised users at a touch of a button.

Key HR Metrical Data & Reports

HR Masters reporting and analytical systems gives businesses key metrical data on critical areas in the system and provides colleagues the reports needed to quickly identify trends and breakdown employee costing across a wide range of areas.

With performance review and training data at your fingertips, isolating training needs, as well as conducting your performance reviews, has never been easier.
